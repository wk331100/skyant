How to send a lucky packet?

When you publish a post, you need to click the lucky packet icon on the publishing page and edit it. You can select the type of coin (BZ/ETH available) and quantity, then enter the number and the amount of lucky packet. Please note that you can only send no more than 100 lucky packets each time, and that the amount of the packet should be no less than 0.1 BZ or 0.0001 ETH.

    How to cancel a lucky packet?

If your lucky packet has been sent out already, you can delete the post. And the unclaimed lucky packet will be refunded to your account 24 hours later;

If your lucky packet has not been sent out yet, you can click the packet icon again to cancel it.

    What if your lucky packet sent out has not been claimed within 24 hours

If your lucky packet has not been claimed within 24 hours totally, the remaining will be refunded to your account. You can view the asset record details in Me-<My Wallet>.

    What if your lucky packet didn’t send out within 24 hours?

If your lucky packet didn’t send out within 24 hours, SkyAnt will cancel your packet, and the asset will be refunded to your account 24 hours later.

    How to get a lucky packet?

On the post homepage, posts included lucky packets are highlighted with a lucky packet icon. After reposting the post with a lucky packet, you will obtain the coin and your asset amount will increase meanwhile.

    How to view the lucky packet details?

You can enter Me-<Lucky Packet>-<History> to view details at any time.
